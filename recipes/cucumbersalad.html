Cucumber salad

Description:

Cucumber salad is the ultimate companion of many a beloved dish, such as salmon. Why does everyone love cucumber salad? Well, why would they not? Cucumber salad is probably the closest humanity will get to absolute precision; the loveable mildness of the humble cucumber, together with the ferocious and exciting vinegar, all mixed together in a wonderfully vibrant collection of sheer brilliance.

To make this simple yet great-tasting dish, simply follow the instructions below.

You will need:

  • One whole cucumber,
  • apple-cider vinegar,
  • sugar and
  • salt.

Steps:

  1. Cut the cucumber into fine slices, and add to a bowl. Mix sugar, water and apple-cider vinegar.
  2. Pour the mixture over the finely sliced cucumber.
  3. Leave be for 5 minutes. Enjoy!
Bon appetit!
