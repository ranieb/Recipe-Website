Fried potatoes

Description:

Fried potatoes are a simply wonderful complement to just about any dish, or a very good single-entree. Complemented with som melted butter, a delightful filet-o-salmon and maybe a fresh cucumber salad.

To make this simple yet great-tasting dish, simply follow the instructions below.

You will need:

  • Potatoes,
  • butter,
  • salt and pepper.

Steps:

  1. Boil the potatoes until tender.
  2. Fry the potatoes in butter, meanwhile adding generous amounts of salt and pepper
  3. Enjoy!
Bon appetit!
